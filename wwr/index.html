Kaevikon Rafting Site Purposes

You are welcome to explore the beautiful river routes of Ibeno and Mbo Local Area of Akwa Ibom State, Nigeria. Ibeno Beach is the longest sandy beach in West Africa, stretching over 45 kilometers along the Atlantic Ocean. Known for its natural beauty with soft sands and rolling waves, the beach is a vibrant local community spot for fishing and a popular tourist attraction offering family-friendly activities and scenic views. It is an important part of the local economy and culture, located near the Qua Iboe River estuary.
